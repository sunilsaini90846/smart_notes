Privacy Policy for Account Note Book

Last Updated: November 18, 2024

Introduction

Account Note Book ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: Account Note Book is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data is stored locally on your device, and we have no access to any information you store in the app.

Information Collection and Use

What We DON'T Collect

Account Note Book does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Account credentials or login information
  • ❌ Notes content (plain or encrypted)
  • ❌ Passwords or sensitive data you store
  • ❌ Device information or identifiers
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Usage analytics or statistics
  • ❌ Crash reports or logs
  • ❌ Any other user data

What Information is Stored

All data created in Account Note Book is stored locally on your device only:

  • Notes: All notes (plain, account, password, bank/card, subscription) are stored in a local database on your device
  • Encrypted Content: When you choose to encrypt a note, it is encrypted locally using AES-256 encryption
  • App Preferences: Basic app settings and preferences stored locally
  • Tags: Custom tags you create for organizing notes

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All data is stored using the Hive database, a secure local storage solution
  • Data files are stored in your device's app-specific storage directory
  • Data is not accessible to other apps on your device (protected by Android/iOS sandboxing)

Encryption

For notes marked as "encrypted":

  • Algorithm: AES-256 in GCM mode (military-grade encryption)
  • Key Derivation: PBKDF2 with SHA-256, 10,000 iterations
  • Password Storage: Passwords are never stored anywhere
  • Decryption: Only possible with the correct password you set
  • Security: Each encrypted note has a unique salt and initialization vector

We have no way to recover your encrypted notes if you forget your password.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

Account Note Book does NOT use any third-party services for:

  • ❌ Analytics
  • ❌ Advertising
  • ❌ Cloud storage
  • ❌ User authentication
  • ❌ Data synchronization
  • ❌ Crash reporting

Data Transmission

  • No Internet Connection Required: The app works completely offline
  • No Data Transmission: No data is ever transmitted over the internet
  • No Servers: We do not operate any servers or backend infrastructure
  • No Cloud Sync: There is no cloud synchronization feature

Legal Requirements

Since we do not collect or have access to any user data, we have nothing to disclose even if legally required to do so.

Your Data Rights

Full Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can access all your notes at any time within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or update any note
  • Delete: You can delete individual notes or all notes
  • Export: You can copy note content to your clipboard

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  1. Delete individual notes within the app, or
  2. Uninstall the app to remove all data

Note: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all notes and data. This action cannot be undone.

Data Portability

Since all data is stored locally:

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it
  • No account creation or login required
  • No data locked behind authentication
  • Copy and paste functionality available for all notes

Children's Privacy

Account Note Book does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, the app can be used by users of all ages. However, we recommend parental guidance for users under 13.

Permissions

Android Permissions

Account Note Book requests minimal permissions:

  • Storage Access (Implicit): Required to store notes in the app's private storage
  • No Network Permission: We do not request or use internet access
  • No Location Permission: We do not access your location
  • No Camera/Microphone: We do not access camera or microphone
  • No Contacts Access: We do not access your contacts

The app is designed to function with the minimum necessary permissions for local data storage.

Data Security

Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  1. Local Storage Only: Data never transmitted over networks
  2. App Sandboxing: Data isolated from other apps by the operating system
  3. Optional Encryption: AES-256 encryption available for sensitive notes
  4. No Cloud Storage: Eliminates risk of data breaches from servers
  5. Open Source Approach: Code is reviewable and transparent

Your Responsibility

To maintain the security of your data:

  • Use strong passwords for encrypted notes
  • Keep your device secure (screen lock, biometrics)
  • Don't share screenshots of sensitive information
  • Keep your device's operating system updated
  • Be cautious when granting device access to others
